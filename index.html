Quality rule

The site must become useful before it earns.

  • Nursery activity screens stay ad-free.
  • Lessons use the correct voice for the learner level.
  • Every lesson needs examples, practice, and answer explanations.
  • Advanced pages must include precise notation and prerequisites.
